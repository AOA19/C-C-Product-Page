About

Cacao and Chocolate got its start making custom made chocolates in Brooklyn, NY in 2021. Handcrafted chocolates made everyday in store, from high quality cacao beans sourced from select cultivators around the country. Choose from our wide assortment of chocolate treats from dark chocolate, chocolate truffles, organic, and gluten-free for every occasion like holidays, birthdays, weddings, and more! Shop in store or check out our online store with easy delivery.
